About Us

Eagle Lens is a Startup business that specializes in Photography and Videography. Through this photography and videography service, one can capture the moment to become more beautiful and valuable. The company to be opened is in Cikarang.

Services

What We Do

By using Eagle Lens, all parties will be facilitated. customers do not bother looking for photographers in the area, especially those who are on vacation and do not know where the photographer is. Of course, this can save on transportation costs, energy, and most importantly time. The photographers that we partner with are also not arbitrary, they have successfully passed the training phase so that customers will not be disappointed by the service provided. Our partners, namely photographers, will also make it very easy to find customers and this makes the photography business come alive and reduces unemployment.

Branding

The reason we only specialize in providing Photographers and Videographers is because Indonesia is a beautiful country with diverse cultures and customs. We guarantee that our every method for all shooting sessions is the best result we produce.

World Fair

Become a large company in the field of photography and videography services that is well known by various groups of society and has high competitiveness.

Location and Venue

Our company is located in Cikarang, where Cikarang itself is an industrial area surrounded by hundreds to thousands of companies, both new and long-established.

Marketing

We have come to the industry with a business concept that will allow us to work with people and companies who have high positions in the country and at the same time with people who are in lower positions and smaller businesses.
